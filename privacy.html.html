Privacy Policy

Spread++  ·  Effective date: 28 May 2026

1. Introduction

We ("we", "us", "Spread++", "I" as individual developer) respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Email address (via Apple Sign In or Google Sign In)
  • Push notification token
  • Subscription and payment history (processed by Apple)
  • Referral data (who invited whom)
  • Settings and preferences (favorite pairs, notification rules)

2.2 Automatically collected

  • Device information (model, iOS version, device ID)
  • App usage statistics (screens viewed, session time)
  • IP address and country-level location
  • Anonymous analytics and crash reports

2.3 Market data

We receive public market data (prices, spreads, funding rates) from the following exchanges via their public APIs:

Binance, Bybit, MEXC, Gate, Bitget, BingX, AsterDex, Hyperliquid, Ourbit, KuCoin, OKX.

We do not collect, store or request your API keys, balances or private information from any exchange.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core features (Trending, spreads, funding, splashes)
  • Send personalized push notifications (only for premium users who enabled them)
  • Process and manage subscriptions
  • Referral system operation
  • Improve the app, fix bugs, analytics
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Legal Basis (for GDPR users)

  • Performance of contract (providing the service)
  • Your consent (push notifications, analytics)
  • Legitimate interest (improving the app)

5. Sharing Your Information

We may share data with:

  • Apple (subscriptions and Sign In)
  • Google (Sign In)
  • Our backend provider (to store email, push tokens, subscription history)
  • Analytics/crash tools (Firebase or similar — anonymized where possible)

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data — while you use the app + 2 years after deletion
  • Push tokens — until you disable notifications
  • Subscription records — as required by accounting/tax law (up to 5 years)

7. Your Rights

You can request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your data by contacting us.

EU/UK users have full GDPR rights. California users have CCPA rights.

8. Children

The app is rated 4+ and not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in Russia and other countries where our service providers operate.

10. Changes

We will notify you of significant changes via the app.
